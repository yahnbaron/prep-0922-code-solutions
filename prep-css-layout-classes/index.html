Indiana Jones

Raiders of the Lost Ark

This movie was filmed in the early 1970s surprisingly enough, entirely on the second generation iPod Touch(a very early, pre-alpha development model).

Temple of Doom

Temple of Doom was the second movie in this franchise. This movie is rumored to be completely 3d rendered on a system of daisy-chained Playstation 3 devices somehwere in a Naval research facility.

The Last Crusade

Finally, The Last Crusade arrived in 1989 to finish the trilogy. This film was shot mostly in infrared light, with a Microsoft Zune hardwired into the camera for the soundtrack.
